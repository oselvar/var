# The library

Maya borrowed *Emma*, due back on June 1st.
She returns it on June 6th and owes a £2.50 late fee — 50p for each day overdue.

Noor borrowed *Kindred*, due back on June 1st.
When she asks to borrow *Beloved* on June 10th, the library refuses: an overdue book blocks new loans.

Ben borrowed *Dune*, due back on June 12th.
When he asks to borrow *Hyperion* on June 10th, the library agrees.
